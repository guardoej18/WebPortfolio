How do you break the pattern?

It will depend from situation to situation and we're all on our own unique journey of awareness. So there's no cookie cutter way for us all. Yet what often helps me is to first notice the pattern. But it's important to pause and acknowledge that we can only make a difference from a space of recognition. And even though this is obvious, here's the difficult part: it will require ACCOUNTABILITY (not blame nor fault) on our part. Accountability heals. In other words, owning our side of the street.

Some periods of our growth are so confusing..

Some periods of our growth are so confusing that we don't even recognize that growth is happening. We may feel hostile or angry or weepy and hysterical, or we may feel depressed. It would never occur to us, unless we stumbled on a book or a pweson who explained to us, that we were in fact in the process of change, of actually becoming larger, spiritually, than we were before.

Feel it. You're human.

There is absolutely nothing wrong with the way you feel. Nothing. Feel it. You're human. This is what being human feels like. So be human. Sometimes this process is messy. Sometimes it's not so clear where you are or where you are headed. And that's perfectly okay. Do not shame yourself anymore for where you are, for what you feel, for who you are and definitely not for the speed at which you are growing. Your growth doesn't have to look like anybody else's.
