About Me

Hello! My name is EJ David and I am passionate when it comes to web designs and web development. I enjoy creating things on the internet. My interest in web development started back in 2022 when I watched one of my colleagues started to design a website. I have had background in some programming languages before, as well as testing websites, yet I decided to enroll in Zuitt bootcamp in order to refresh my knowledge and to sharpen my skills regarding web development. Here are some of the skills I acquired during the bootcamp:

  • HTML
  • CSS
  • Bootstrap
  • Responsive Web Design
  • Front-end Development
  • Javascript

Regarding about my background, I studied electronics engineering and I've worked in the last few years in different firms such as manufacturing and telecommunications. I decided to switch career path since I believe that the web development industry is booming with opportunity. Aside from that, it has higher salary potential and growth flexibility. This career scope has absolutely its ups and downs and is challenging but I believe in the end it will be all worth it.
